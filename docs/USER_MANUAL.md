# Student Buddy App - User Manual

## Welcome to Student Buddy!

Student Buddy is your personal companion designed to help you manage your daily life, focus on your goals, and understand your habits. It is built to support Executive Function and Self-Regulation, making it a great tool for anyone looking to bring more structure and awareness to their day.

## Core Features

### 1. Task Management
Stay on top of your to-do list without the overwhelm.
- **Chunking**: Break large tasks into smaller, manageable subtasks.
- **Prioritization**: Clearly see what's Urgent, High, or Low priority.
- **Time Estimation**: Set estimated times for tasks to help with time management.

### 2. Focus Tools (Pomodoro)
Use the built-in timer to structure your work sessions.
- **Work/Break Cycles**: The app supports the Pomodoro technique (e.g., 25 minutes work, 5 minutes break) to keep your mind fresh.

### 3. Life Tracking & Analysis
Understand how different factors affect your well-being.
- **Track Inputs**: Log daily metrics like Sleep duration, Caffeine intake, and Exercise.
- **Track Outputs**: Log how you feel (Mood, Energy, Focus).
- **Find Correlations**: The app analyzes your data to show connections (e.g., "I have better focus when I sleep 8 hours").

### 4. Reflection & Journaling
- **Daily Journal**: Set intentions for the day and reflect on what went well.
- **Strategy Toolbox**: Access guides and coping mechanisms.

## Getting Started

1.  **Dashboard**: When you open the app, you'll see your Dashboard. This gives you a quick overview of your current tasks and active timers.
2.  **Tracking**: Navigate to the Tracker section to log your daily stats. Try to do this consistently for the best insights!
3.  **Journal**: Use the Journal tab to clear your mind or plan your day.

## Tips for Success
- **Be Consistent**: The more data you log, the smarter the app becomes at finding patterns.
- **Start Small**: Don't try to use every feature at once. Start with simple task lists or just tracking sleep and mood.
- **Review**: Check your correlation insights weekly to adjust your habits.
