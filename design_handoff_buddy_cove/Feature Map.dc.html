Buddy — what goes where
Every feature in the codebase → my proposal for the new UI. Tell me where you disagree.
FRONT — part of the daily loop, always visible ONE TAP — lives in Browse, full screen when opened FOLDED — merged into another flow, not its own screen QUIET — settings / power tools, tucked away
The daily loop (front)
FRONT Morning check-in (the gate) — keeps the original light-routine steps: Comms (mail/messages checklist) → Log yesterday (quick mood + energy) → Plan today (meds/protocols, one-word intention, pick 3 tasks). Always first thing every day; skippable manually only.
FRONT Now screen — whale + today's three picks + capture + streak chips. Close-day button appears when picks are done or it's evening.
FRONT Midday reset — original feature, kept as a gentle card on Now in the afternoon ("How's it going?" — review picks, adjust). Not a gate; dismissible.
FRONT Close day / evening reflection — celebration + the original reflection basics folded in: mood & energy tap, one journal line, streak bump. Full journal/skills/goals stays one tap deeper.
FRONT Capture + triage inbox — capture anything anywhere; inbox sorts one item at a time (Today / Later / School). AI auto-sorting stays invisible in the background.
FRONT Survival day — one toggle at the morning gate: 1 pick instead of 3, light everything, notifications hushed. Original capacity feature, made friendlier.
One tap away (Browse)
ONE TAP Tasks — full list with the original chips (Urgent / Today / Someday / Quick wins / Done), snooze, "split this?" on stuck tasks, someday shown one-a-day.
ONE TAP School — classes, assignments & deadlines, weekly schedule. Assignments already mirror into tasks, so school work shows up in picks automatically.
ONE TAP Calendar — day timeline with events + timed picks (Google Calendar sync stays).
ONE TAP Health — daily check-in + simple trends. The deep analysis, correlations and experiments stay, but behind a second tap ("Explore my data").
ONE TAP Notes — quick note + smart notes list.
ONE TAP Focus timer — pomodoro, reachable from a task ("focus on this") and from Browse.
ONE TAP Toolbox & checklists — coping tools and checklists; calendar-triggered checklists ("work day → work checklist") surface on Now only when triggered.
Folded into other flows
FOLDED Protocols (meds/supplements) — becomes a step inside the morning check-in, not its own page. Editing protocols → Health.
FOLDED Reflection / journal / goals / skills — the essentials (mood, energy, one line) live in close-day; the full page stays reachable from close-day → "more reflection".
FOLDED Urgent scheduling — sorting something as "Urgent" immediately asks when + what prep, then writes to calendar. A moment inside triage, not a screen.
FOLDED Stats & insights — week bars, streaks, routine-kept count live behind "More" on Now. No dashboard screen; numbers stay gentle and few.
Quiet (settings & power tools)
QUIET Notifications — anchors, quiet hours, per-task reminders → all under Me. The app never shows badges or counts on the nav.
QUIET AI power tools — task splitter, AI organize, task types, routine editor → behind a "⋯ tools" button on the Tasks screen. AI assistant chat stays as Capture's smart brain, not a separate chat UI.
QUIET Dev & internals — assistant dev panel, push health, rules/learnings, account details → Me → advanced. Never on the daily path.
